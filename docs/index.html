How It Works

From call to booked inspection in under 3 minutes

Maria handles the full qualification flow — no scripts, no prompts, just a natural conversation.

1

Maria answers instantly — no hold music, no voicemail. She greets in English and switches to Spanish the moment she detects it.

She asks about pest type, urgency, property size, and readiness to book — all woven naturally into conversation, not read from a list.
