# Green Shield Pest Control — 90-Second Demo Script

**Scenario:** Customer calls about a scorpion problem. Maria qualifies the lead, switches to Spanish when the customer prefers, scores it 9/10, and books a same-day inspection.

---

## Setup (Before Demo)

- Dashboard open at `http://localhost:5002`
- Phone number ready to call
- Audience can see the dashboard (ideally on screen share or projector)

---

## The Script

### [RING — Maria answers immediately]

**Maria (English):**
> "Thank you for calling Green Shield Pest Control, protecting Texas homes since 2015. This is Maria — how can I help you today?"

---

### [Customer — play this role yourself or have a volunteer]

**Customer (English):**
> "Hi, yeah — I found a scorpion in my bathroom this morning and I'm pretty freaked out. I have a two-year-old at home."

---

**Maria:**
> "Oh, I completely understand — that is scary, especially with a little one at home. You did the right thing calling us. Can I get your name?"

**Customer:** "It's Roberto."

**Maria:** "Hi Roberto. So tell me — was this just the one scorpion, or have you been seeing them around the house?"

**Customer:** "Bueno... actually, found two more in the garage last week. ¿Habla español?"

---

### [Maria switches to Spanish seamlessly]

**Maria (Spanish):**
> "¡Por supuesto, con mucho gusto! Roberto, eso me preocupa un poco — tres alacranes en una semana es una señal de que pueden haber más escondidos. ¿Su casa es propia o rentada?"

**Customer (Spanish):** "Es mía. Tenemos como tres mil pies cuadrados aquí en San Antonio."

**Maria:**
> "Perfecto. Y con un niño pequeño en casa, ¿estaría buscando que mandemos a alguien hoy mismo, o prefiere esperar?"

**Customer:** "Hoy si puede, sí. Lo antes posible."

**Maria:**
> "Entiendo perfectamente. ¿Y ha usado Green Shield antes, o sería su primera vez con nosotros?"

**Customer:** "Primera vez."

---

### [Maria routes the lead — score 9/10]

**Maria:**
> "Roberto, basándome en todo lo que me ha compartido — tres alacranes en una semana, un niño pequeño en casa, una propiedad de buen tamaño — esto es exactamente el tipo de situación en que podemos ayudarle hoy mismo. Tenemos inspecciones gratuitas y servicio el mismo día para emergencias como la suya."

> "¿Le funciona esta tarde entre la una y las cuatro? Puedo coordinar a Técnico Martínez para que pase a su casa."

**Customer:** "Sí, perfecto."

**Maria:** "Excelente. ¿Me puede dar su correo electrónico para mandarle la confirmación?"

**Customer:** "roberto.sanchez@gmail.com"

**Maria:**
> "Listo, Roberto. Técnico Martínez estará ahí esta tarde. Le vamos a mandar una confirmación al correo. ¿Hay algo más en lo que le pueda ayudar?"

**Customer:** "No, muchas gracias."

**Maria:**
> "De nada, Roberto. Vamos a resolver esto para usted y su familia. ¡Que tenga un buen día, estamos para servirle!"

## Key Demo Moments to Highlight

1. **Instant answer** — No hold music, no voicemail, Maria picks up immediately
2. **Language detection** — Customer says "¿Habla español?" and Maria switches instantly, no friction
3. **Natural conversation** — Maria doesn't read from a script; she adapts to what Roberto already shared
4. **Urgency recognition** — She catches "two-year-old at home" and reflects it back, showing empathy
5. **Live dashboard** — Lead appears in real time while still on the call (show this on screen)
6. **Score transparency** — Walk the audience through the 9/10 breakdown after the call

## Presenter Talk Track (Between Moments)

**Opening (before call):**
> "Right now, the average pest control company misses 30% of after-hours calls. Those go straight to voicemail — and most of those customers just call the next company. Maria fixes that. She answers every call, any time, in English and Spanish. Let's watch."

**After language switch:**
> "Did you catch that? The customer switched to Spanish and Maria followed immediately — no 'please press 2 for Spanish,' no awkward pause. That's what feels like a real person."

**After booking:**
> "Nine out of ten. Scorpions, small child, same-day urgency, new customer. Maria identified every signal and booked the inspection. The technician gets a notification. The customer gets a confirmation email. Nobody dropped the ball."

**Closing (showing dashboard):**
> "This is what the Green Shield team sees. Every lead scored, categorized, ready for follow-up. Maria doesn't replace your team — she makes sure every call they get is already qualified."
