# AXIOM Voice Agent — Pest Control Demo

A bilingual (EN/ES) AI receptionist for pest control companies. Answers calls 24/7, identifies the pest problem, assesses urgency, checks service area, and books appointments.

## What It Does
1. Answers the phone instantly — no hold music, no voicemail
2. Identifies the pest problem and urgency level
3. Checks service area availability
4. Books the appointment
5. Sends text confirmation/reminders
6. Logs the lead with full details

## Why Pest Control Companies Need This
- Average missed call costs $150-500 in lost revenue
- Peak season (spring/summer) = 200+ calls/day
- Most companies miss 20-30% of calls during busy hours
- After-hours calls go to voicemail (leads lost to competitors)

## Setup
Uses same Vapi.ai infrastructure as the law firm demo.
- Copy `system_prompt.txt` into your Vapi assistant config
- Configure availability calendar integration
- Set service area zip codes
- Connect to CRM for lead logging

## Customization Per Client
- Company name (replace "GreenShield Pest Control")
- Service area (zip codes)
- Pricing (free inspection vs. paid)
- Warranty terms (30/60/90 days)
- Business hours and emergency availability
- Voice selection (male/female, accent preference)
